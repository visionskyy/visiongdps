About Vision GDPS

Vision GDPS is a fun, player-friendly game where you can create and share custom levels. Join our community to stay updated and get involved!

Game Rules

    • 1️⃣ Be respectful.
      No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
    • 2️⃣ No spamming.
      Don’t flood channels with messages, links, or repeated posts.
    • 3️⃣ Keep content appropriate.
      No NSFW, illegal, or disturbing content.
    • 4️⃣ No advertising.
      Don’t promote other servers, sites, or self-promo without permission.
    • 5️⃣ Use channels properly.
      Post in the correct channel, follow channel topics.
    • 6️⃣ No cheating or exploiting.
      Respect the game — no hacks, cheats, or exploits in Vision GDPS.
    • 7️⃣ Listen to staff.
      Follow instructions from mods/admins.
    • 8️⃣ No pretending to be a moderator.
      Pretending to be a Mod in game or in the server You will get a timeout from the server chat, if repeated you will get KICKED/BANNED.
    • 9️⃣ Have fun!
      Be chill — we’re here to play and vibe.
